Display Over Other Apps

This allows the floating timer to appear on top of every other app on Android. When converted to a native app, it shows as a persistent overlay.

For Native App (Android):

1. Convert using Cordova/Capacitor (config.xml included)

2. Users grant: Settings > Apps > PlanAlarm > Display over other apps
