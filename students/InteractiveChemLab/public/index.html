How to Use the Simulator

Getting Started

  1. Read the question carefully at the top of the page
  2. Drag apparatus from the shelf onto the work surface
  3. Click on the burette to access controls for rinsing, filling, and titration
  4. Follow the procedure steps shown on the right panel
  5. Record your burette readings in the titration table
  6. Complete the calculations as required
  7. Submit your practical for grading

Controls

  • Drag & Drop: Move apparatus onto the workspace
  • Burette Controls: Click burette to rinse, fill, and release acid
  • Flask Shake: Automatically shakes when acid is added
  • Color Change: Watch for indicator color change at endpoint
