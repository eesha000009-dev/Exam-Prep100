Punctuation

Rules for commas, semicolons, colons, apostrophes and quotation marks.

Worked example

Add commas: "After the show we went home."

Solution: "After the show, we went home."

Practice

  1. Insert commas: "My brother who lives abroad is visiting."
  2. Use apostrophe: show possessive for "the girls book"

Brief answers

  1. "My brother, who lives abroad, is visiting."
  2. "the girl's book"
