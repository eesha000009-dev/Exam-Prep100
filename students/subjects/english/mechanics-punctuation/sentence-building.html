Sentence Building

Combine clauses to form coherent, well-punctuated sentences with appropriate transitions.

Worked example

Combine: "She was late. She missed the bus."

Solution: "She was late because she missed the bus."

Practice

  1. Combine two short sentences into a compound sentence.
  2. Use a subordinating conjunction to make a complex sentence.
