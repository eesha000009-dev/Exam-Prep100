Antonyms, Synonyms & Homonyms

Improve precision in word choice by practicing similar and opposite words; watch for homonyms.

Worked example

Synonym for "quick": "fast". Antonym: "slow".

Practice

  1. Give synonyms for: "happy", "difficult"
  2. Show difference: "their" / "there" / "they're"

Brief answers

  1. happy = glad; difficult = hard
  2. their = possessive; there = place; they're = they are
