Sentence Classification

Distinguish simple, compound, complex and compound-complex sentences.

Worked example

Classify: "I came, I saw, I conquered."

Solution: Compound sentence (coordinated independent clauses).

Practice

  1. Label: "When the bell rang, the students left."
  2. Identify compound-complex example.

Brief answers

  1. Complex (subordinate clause + main clause).
  2. Any sentence with two independent clauses and at least one subordinate clause.
