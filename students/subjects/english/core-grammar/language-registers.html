Language Registers

Distinguish formal, informal, academic and colloquial styles and when to use each.

Worked example

Rewrite informally: "I regret to inform you that I cannot attend."

Solution: "Sorry, I can't make it."

Practice

  1. Convert a sentence to formal tone.
  2. Identify register in a short paragraph.

Brief answers

  1. Example formal rewrite: "I regret that I am unable to attend."
  2. Answers will vary by paragraph.
