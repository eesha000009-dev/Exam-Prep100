Concord & Verbals

Subject–verb agreement with tricky cases; roles of infinitives, gerunds and participles.

Worked example

Choose verb: "Neither of the answers (is/are) correct."

Solution: "Neither of the answers is correct." (subject is 'neither').

Practice

  1. Decide agreement: "A bouquet of flowers (was/were) on the table."
  2. Identify function: "Swimming is good exercise." (gerund as subject)

Brief answers

  1. "was"
  2. Gerund functioning as subject.
