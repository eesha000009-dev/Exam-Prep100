Word Classes

Short guide to nouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs, prepositions and conjunctions with quick usage tips.

Worked example

Classify words: "Swiftly the small cat chased the frightened mouse."

Solution: Swiftly — adverb; the — article; small — adjective; cat — noun; chased — verb; the — article; frightened — adjective; mouse — noun.

Practice

  1. Identify the verbs and adverbs in: "They will quickly finish the task."
  2. Pick the adjectives in: "A bright red kite soared high."

Brief answers

  1. Verbs: "will finish"; Adverb: "quickly"
  2. Adjectives: "bright", "red"
