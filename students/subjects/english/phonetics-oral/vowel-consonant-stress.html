Vowel & Consonant Sounds, Stress

Monophthongs/diphthongs, plosives, fricatives and word/sentence stress basics.

Worked example

Identify stress: "PHOtograph" vs "phoTOgraphy"

Solution: Primary stress on first syllable in 'photograph', on second syllable in 'photography'.

Practice

  1. Mark stress for: "record" (noun vs verb)
  2. Identify sound type: /p/ (plosive) vs /f/ (fricative)

Brief answers

  1. Noun: REcord (stress on first); Verb: reCORD (stress on second)
  2. /p/ = plosive; /f/ = fricative
