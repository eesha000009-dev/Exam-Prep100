Minimal-pair Drills

Pairs like ship/sheep, bit/beat to train perception and production.

Worked example

Listen and say: ship / sheep — what changes?

Solution: Vowel length and quality: /ɪ/ vs /iː/.

Practice

  1. Minimal pair: "pat" vs "bat" (different initial consonant)
  2. Practice: produce and record both words, note differences.

Brief answers

  1. Pat /pæt/ vs Bat /bæt/
  2. Answers: note voicing difference in initial consonant.
