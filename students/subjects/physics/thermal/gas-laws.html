Gas Laws

Ideal gas law PV = nRT connects pressure, volume and temperature. Also consider Boyle's and Charles' laws in limiting cases.

Practice

  1. A 1.0 L gas at 1 atm is compressed isothermally to 0.5 L. Find the final pressure.

Solution

Isothermal compression: P1V1 = P2V2 → P2 = P1V1/V2 = 1×1/0.5 = 2 atm.
