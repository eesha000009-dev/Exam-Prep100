Practice task

Design a simple experiment to measure g using a pendulum. List required equipment, steps and key sources of error.

Brief checklist / hints

  • Equipment: string, bob, stopwatch, meter rule, clamp.
  • Measure period for several lengths; use T = 2π√(L/g) and linearize T^2 vs L to reduce random error.
