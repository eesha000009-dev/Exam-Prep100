Practice

Given repeated length measurements: 1.02, 1.00, 1.03, estimate the mean and standard uncertainty (use sample stdev/√n).

Brief solution

Mean ≈ 1.0167 m; sample stdev ≈ 0.0153 m → uncertainty ≈ 0.0153/√3 ≈ 0.0088 m.
