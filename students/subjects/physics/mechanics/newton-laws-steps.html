Learning Steps

Key Concept

An object will remain at rest or in uniform motion in a straight line unless acted upon by an external force.

Understanding Inertia

Inertia is the resistance of an object to any change in its state of motion. This includes:

  • Changes in speed
  • Changes in direction
  • Starting from rest
  • Coming to a stop

Real-World Examples

Example 1: Car Braking

Passengers lean forward when a car suddenly brakes due to their inertia.

Example 2: Table Cloth Trick

Objects on a tablecloth stay in place when the cloth is quickly pulled away.
