Equilibrium of Forces

Understanding static and dynamic equilibrium in physical systems

Conditions for Equilibrium

Translational Equilibrium

  • • ΣF = 0 (Net force is zero)
  • • ΣFx = 0 (x-direction)
  • • ΣFy = 0 (y-direction)
  • • No linear acceleration

Rotational Equilibrium

  • • Στ = 0 (Net torque is zero)
  • • Choose convenient pivot point
  • • Consider all forces' moments
  • • No angular acceleration

Types of Equilibrium

Stable Equilibrium

  • • Returns to original position
  • • Minimum potential energy
  • • Example: Pendulum at rest
  • • Self-correcting

Unstable Equilibrium

  • • Moves away from position
  • • Maximum potential energy
  • • Example: Pencil on point
  • • Any disturbance critical

Neutral Equilibrium

  • • Stays in new position
  • • Constant potential energy
  • • Example: Ball on flat surface
  • • No restoring force

Practice Problems

    A ladder leans against a frictionless wall. Find the forces at both ends for equilibrium.
