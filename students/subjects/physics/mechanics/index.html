Mechanics

Understanding motion, forces, energy, and their interactions in physical systems

Why Study Mechanics?

Mechanics forms the foundation of classical physics and explains how objects move and interact. From the motion of planets to the workings of machines, mechanical principles govern much of the physical world around us.
