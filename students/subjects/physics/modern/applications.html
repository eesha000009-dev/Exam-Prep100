Modern Physics Applications

Lasers, semiconductors and basic applications of quantum ideas.

Practice questions

  1. Give two applications of lasers in measurement and medicine.
  2. Explain briefly how a semiconductor diode allows current in one direction only.

Brief solutions

  1. Measurement: interferometry for small distances; Medicine: laser surgery and ophthalmic procedures.
  2. A p–n junction forms a barrier that allows charge carriers to flow under forward bias but not under reverse bias because of the depletion region.
