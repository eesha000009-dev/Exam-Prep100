Interference & Diffraction

Young's double-slit interference: fringe spacing x = λD/d. Single-slit diffraction gives central maximum width depending on slit width.

Practice

  1. Compute fringe spacing for λ=500 nm, D=1 m, d=0.5 mm.

Solution

x = λD/d = 5e-7 × 1 / 5e-4 = 1e-3 m = 1 mm.
