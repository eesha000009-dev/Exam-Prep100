Lenses & Mirrors

Lens formula: 1/f = 1/u + 1/v. Sign conventions: convex has positive focal length, concave negative (simple treatment here).

Practice

  1. An object is 30 cm from a convex lens of focal length 10 cm. Find image distance.

Solution

1/v = 1/f − 1/u = 1/10 − 1/30 = (3−1)/30 = 2/30 → v = 15 cm.
