Practice

  1. Describe why pure water is a poor conductor while salt water conducts well.
  2. Explain in one paragraph how ion mobility affects conductivity.

Solutions (brief)

  1. Pure water has very few free ions; salt dissociates into ions which carry charge.
  2. Higher ion mobility and concentration → higher conductivity; larger ions or viscous media reduce mobility.
