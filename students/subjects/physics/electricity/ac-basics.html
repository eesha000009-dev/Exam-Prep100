AC Concepts

Alternating current basics, frequency, RMS values and a mention of transformers and reactance.

RMS values

For a sinusoidal voltage V_peak, V_rms = V_peak / √2. Power using RMS values: P = V_rms I_rms cosφ.

Transformers (qualitative)

Step-up and step-down transformers, turns ratio and ideal transformer relation: Vp/Vs = Np/Ns.

Practice

  1. Compute Vrms for 311 V peak (mains in some countries).
  2. Given Vp/Vs = Np/Ns, if a transformer has 1000 turns primary and 100 turns secondary with 230 V primary, find secondary voltage.

Solutions (brief)

  1. Vrms = 311/√2 ≈ 220 V.
  2. Vs = Vp × (Ns/Np) = 230 × (100/1000) = 23 V.
