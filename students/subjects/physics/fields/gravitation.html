Gravitation & Orbits

Newton's law of gravitation: F = G m1 m2 / r². Circular orbit speed v = √(GM/r) and escape velocity v_esc = √(2GM/r).

Practice questions

  1. Calculate the orbital speed of a low Earth orbit satellite at r = 6.8×10^6 m (use GM = 3.986×10^14 m^3/s^2).
  2. Find the escape velocity from Earth's surface (r ≈ 6.37×10^6 m).

Brief solutions

  1. v = √(GM/r) ≈ √(3.986e14 / 6.8e6) ≈ 7670 m/s.
  2. v_esc = √(2GM/r) ≈ √(2×3.986e14 / 6.37e6) ≈ 11200 m/s.
