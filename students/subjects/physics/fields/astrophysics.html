Astrophysics Overview

Intro to stars, HR diagram, and basic stellar lifecycle concepts at a high level.

Practice questions

  1. Describe where main-sequence stars sit on the HR diagram and why.
  2. Give a brief sequence of stellar evolution for a Sun-like star.

Brief solutions

  1. Main-sequence stars are on a diagonal from hot/luminous to cool/dim because they are burning hydrogen in their cores; hotter stars are more luminous.
  2. Sun-like: protostar → main sequence (H burning) → red giant → planetary nebula → white dwarf.
