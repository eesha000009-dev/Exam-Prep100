Nutrition, Digestion & Energy Capture

Why Study This?

Energy flow through organisms (capturing, processing food) underpins physiology and ecology.

Topics

  • Nutrition / Food substances (carbohydrates, proteins, lipids, vitamins, minerals)
  • Photosynthesis (light & dark reactions, limiting factors)
  • Feeding mechanisms / Digestive system (human and comparative overview)

Key Practicals

  • Test for food substances (Benedict, Biuret, iodine)
  • Photosynthesis experiments (leaf starch test, effect of light intensity/CO₂)
  • Dissection or model of digestive tract
