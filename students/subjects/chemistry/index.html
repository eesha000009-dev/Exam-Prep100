Chemistry

Structured learning order — start with Foundations of Chemistry.

1. Foundations of Chemistry

Basic atomic theory, mole concept and simple chemical calculations.

2. States of Matter & Gas Laws

Solids, liquids, gases and the laws governing gas behaviour.

3. Periodicity & Bonding

Periodic trends and different bonding types.

4. Chemical Calculations & Stoichiometry

Mole calculations, balancing equations and stoichiometry.

5. Acids, Bases, Salts & Redox

Acids and bases, titrations, buffers and basic redox concepts.

6. Electrochemistry

Electrolysis, cells and electrode potentials.

7. Energy, Rates & Equilibria

Thermochemistry, kinetics and chemical equilibrium.

8. Inorganic & Analytical Chemistry

Element families, extraction, and analytical techniques.

9. Organic Chemistry

Hydrocarbons, functional groups and biomolecules.

10. Applied Chemistry & Environment

Industrial processes, pollution and sustainability.

11. Nuclear Chemistry

Radioactivity, nuclear reactions and applications.

12. Chemistry Practicals

General Skills, Quantitative Analysis, Qualitative Analysis.
