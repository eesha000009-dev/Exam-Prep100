Gas Laws (Boyle & Charles)

Statements and simple worked examples: Boyle's law (P ∝ 1/V) and Charles's law (V ∝ T).

Worked example

If 2.0 L of gas at 1.0 atm is compressed to 0.5 L at constant temperature, what is the new pressure? Using P1V1=P2V2 → P2= P1V1/V2 = 1.0×2.0/0.5 = 4.0 atm.
