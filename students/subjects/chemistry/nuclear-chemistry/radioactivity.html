Radioactivity: Types & Properties

Overview of alpha, beta and gamma emissions, half-life and detection.

Worked example

If a sample has a half-life of 5 years, after 15 years what fraction remains? Answer: (1/2)^(15/5) = (1/2)^3 = 1/8.
