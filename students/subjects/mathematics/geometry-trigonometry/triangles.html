Triangles

Triangle types, properties, Pythagoras' theorem and basic congruence/similarity concepts.

Worked example

Right triangle with legs 3 and 4: hypotenuse = 5 (3-4-5 triple).

Practice

  1. Find hypotenuse for right triangle with legs 5 and 12.
  2. Given triangle with sides 6,8,10, show it's right-angled.

Brief solutions

  1. √(5^2+12^2)=13.
  2. 6^2+8^2=36+64=100=10^2, so right-angled.
