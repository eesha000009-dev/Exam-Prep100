Triangle Basics

Angles in a triangle sum to 180°. Types: equilateral, isosceles, scalene. Right triangle uses Pythagoras: a² + b² = c².

Worked Examples

  1. Find hypotenuse when legs 6 and 8 → c = 10.
  2. Isosceles triangle: sides 5,5,6 → height = √(5² - 3²) = 4.
