Bearings

Compass bearings, converting between bearings and standard positional angles, and solving simple navigation problems.

Practice

  1. A ship sails on bearing 045°. What direction is this in standard angle notation?
  2. Find bearing from A to B given coordinates (brief outline).

Brief solutions

  1. 045° is northeast; standard angle measured from East CCW is 360-45=315° or depending on convention note as 45° from North clockwise.
