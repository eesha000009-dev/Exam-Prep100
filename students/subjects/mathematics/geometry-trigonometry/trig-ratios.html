Definitions

sin θ = opposite / hypotenuse
cos θ = adjacent / hypotenuse
tan θ = opposite / adjacent = sin θ / cos θ

Exact values

sin30 = 1/2, cos30 = √3/2, tan30 = 1/√3
sin45 = √2/2, cos45 = √2/2, tan45 = 1
sin60 = √3/2, cos60 = 1/2, tan60 = √3
