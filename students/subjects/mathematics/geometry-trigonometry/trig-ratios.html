Trigonometric Ratios

Definition of sine, cosine, tangent for right-angled triangles; basic identities and exact values for special angles.

Worked example

For 3-4-5 triangle: sinθ = opposite/hypotenuse = 3/5, cosθ = 4/5, tanθ = 3/4.

Practice

  1. Find sin30°, cos60° (exact values).
  2. Compute tan45°.

Brief solutions

  1. sin30° = 1/2, cos60° = 1/2.
  2. tan45° = 1.
