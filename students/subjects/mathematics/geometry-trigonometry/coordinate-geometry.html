Coordinate Geometry

Distance formula, midpoint, gradient of a line and equation of a straight line in slope-intercept form.

Practice

  1. Find distance between (1,2) and (4,6).
  2. Find slope of line through (0,0) and (3,3).

Brief solutions

  1. Distance = √[(3)^2+(4)^2] = 5.
  2. Slope = (3-0)/(3-0)=1.
