Distance formula

Distance between (x1,y1) and (x2,y2): √[(x2-x1)² + (y2-y1)²].

Midpoint

Midpoint: ((x1+x2)/2, (y1+y2)/2).

Slope & Equation of a line

Slope (m) = (y2 - y1) / (x2 - x1). Line equation: y = mx + c (slope-intercept) or point-slope form.
