Mensuration

Formulas for area and perimeter of common shapes, surface area and volume of prisms, cylinders, cones and spheres.

Practice

  1. Find volume of cylinder r=3, h=5.
  2. Area of rectangle 4 by 7.

Brief solutions

  1. V = πr^2h = π·9·5 = 45π.
  2. Area = 28.
