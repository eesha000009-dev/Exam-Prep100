Key Formulas

Circle
Area = πr², Circumference = 2πr
Rectangle
Area = l × w
Triangle
Area = ½ × base × height
Cylinder
Volume = πr²h

Worked Examples

  1. Circle radius 7 cm → area = π×7² = 154 cm² (use π≈3.14)
  2. Cylinder r=3 cm, h=10 cm → volume = π×9×10 ≈ 282.6 cm³

Practice

1) Circle r = 5 cm. Area?
2) Cylinder r=4, h=8. Volume?

Brief solutions

  1. V = πr^2h = π·9·5 = 45π.
  2. Area = 28.
