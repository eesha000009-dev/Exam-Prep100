Circle basics

Key terms: radius (r), diameter (d = 2r), chord, tangent, sector and arc. Area = πr², circumference = 2πr.

Important Theorems

  • Angle in a semicircle is a right angle (90°).
  • Equal angles in the same segment subtend equal arcs.
  • Tangent and radius are perpendicular at the point of contact.

Area & Sector

Circle area: A = πr². Sector area for angle θ (degrees): A = (θ/360)·πr².
