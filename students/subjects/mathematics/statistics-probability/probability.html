Core ideas

Probability is a number between 0 and 1 that measures how likely an event is. P(A)= favourable outcomes / total outcomes for equally likely cases.

Venn diagram — events

Overlap shows intersection P(A∩B). Use inclusion-exclusion: P(A∪B)=P(A)+P(B)-P(A∩B).

Probability tree (conditional)

Trees help list sequential outcomes and calculate combined probabilities.
