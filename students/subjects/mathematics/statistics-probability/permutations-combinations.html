Permutations & Combinations

Counting techniques: factorial notation, permutations (ordered) and combinations (unordered) with simple examples.

Practice

  1. How many ways to arrange 3 letters from ABCD?
  2. Choose 2 students from a group of 5 (combinations).

Brief solutions

  1. Permutations P(4,3)=4×3×2=24.
  2. Combinations C(5,2)=10.
