Modular Arithmetic

Congruences, computations mod n, applications to remainders and cyclic patterns.

Worked example

Find 7^3 mod 10 → 343 mod 10 = 3.

Practice

  1. Compute 15 ≡ ? (mod 4).
  2. What is (3 + 9) mod 5?

Brief solutions

  1. 15 ≡ 3 (mod 4).
  2. (3+9)=12 ≡ 2 (mod 5).
