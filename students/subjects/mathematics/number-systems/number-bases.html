Number Bases

Binary, octal, decimal and hexadecimal; converting between bases and basic arithmetic in different bases.

Worked example

Convert binary 1101 to decimal: 1×8+1×4+0×2+1×1 = 13.

Practice

  1. Convert decimal 255 to hexadecimal.
  2. Add binary: 1011 + 1101.

Brief solutions

  1. 255 = FF (hex).
  2. 1011 + 1101 = (carry) → 11000.
