Linear Equations & Inequalities

Solving single-variable linear equations and simple inequalities; graphical interpretation of solutions.

Worked example

Solve: 3x - 5 = 16 → 3x = 21 → x = 7.

Practice

  1. Solve: 2x + 3 = 11.
  2. Solve inequality: 5 - 2x > 1.

Brief solutions

  1. x = 4.
  2. 5 - 2x > 1 → -2x > -4 → x < 2.
