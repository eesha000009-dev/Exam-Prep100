Polynomials

Definitions: degree, leading coefficient; addition, subtraction, multiplication and basic division of polynomials.

Worked example

Multiply: (x+2)(x-3) = x^2 - x - 6.

Practice

  1. Find degree of 4x^3 + x - 7.
  2. Multiply: (2x+1)(x+3).

Brief solutions

  1. Degree = 3.
  2. 2x^2 + 7x + 3.
