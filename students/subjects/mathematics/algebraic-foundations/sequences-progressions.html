Sequences & Progressions

Arithmetic and geometric progressions: nth-term formulas, sum of n terms and simple problem-solving.

Worked example

AP with first term 3 and difference 2: nth term a_n = 3 + (n-1)2.

Practice

  1. Find 5th term of GP with a=2, r=3.
  2. Sum of first 4 terms of AP with a=1, d=2.

Brief solutions

  1. GP term: a r^{n-1} = 2 × 3^{4} = 162.
  2. AP sum: n/2 (2a + (n-1)d) = 4/2 (2 + 3×2) = 2×8 = 16.
