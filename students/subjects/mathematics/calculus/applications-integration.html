Area & Accumulation

Definite integrals compute accumulated quantities like area, distance, and total change: ∫_a^b f(x) dx.

Visual — area between curve and axis

Area under y = x from 0 to 2 equals 2 (triangle area).

Worked examples

  1. Area under y = x from 0 to 2: ∫_0^2 x dx = 2.
  2. Displacement from v(t)=3t^2 over [0,2] = 8.
  3. Area ∫_0^3 2x dx = [x^2]_0^3 = 9.
  4. Area ∫_0^2 x^2 dx = [x^3/3]_0^2 = 8/3.
