Applications of Integration

Use definite integrals to compute areas, accumulated quantities and simple kinematics (displacement from velocity).

Worked example

Area under y = x from 0 to 2: ∫_0^2 x dx = [x^2/2]_0^2 = 2.

Practice

  1. Find area under y = 2x from 0 to 3.
  2. Given v(t)=3t^2, find displacement from t=0 to t=2.

Brief solutions

  1. ∫_0^3 2x dx = [x^2]_0^3 = 9.
  2. Displacement = ∫_0^2 3t^2 dt = [t^3]_0^2 = 8.
