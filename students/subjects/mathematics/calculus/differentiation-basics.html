Differentiation Basics

Definition of derivative as rate of change and gradient of tangent; power rule and basic derivatives of polynomials.

Worked example

If f(x)=x^3, f'(x)=3x^2. So at x=2 gradient is 12.

Practice

  1. Differentiate y = 5x^4.
  2. Find derivative of y = x^2 + 3x at x = 1.

Brief solutions

  1. dy/dx = 20x^3.
  2. dy/dx = 2x + 3 → at x=1 is 5.
