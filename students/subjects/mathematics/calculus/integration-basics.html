Integration Basics

Antiderivatives and basic rules: power rule for integration, definite vs indefinite integrals and interpretation as area under a curve.

Worked example

∫ x^2 dx = x^3/3 + C.

Practice

  1. Integrate ∫ 3x^2 dx.
  2. Evaluate ∫_0^1 x dx.

Brief solutions

  1. ∫ 3x^2 dx = x^3 + C.
  2. ∫_0^1 x dx = [x^2/2]_0^1 = 1/2.
