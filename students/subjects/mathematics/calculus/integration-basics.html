Antiderivatives & area

Integration reverses differentiation. ∫ x^n dx = x^{n+1}/(n+1) + C. Definite integrals compute area under curves.

Visual — area under curve

Area between curve and x-axis from a to b equals definite integral ∫_a^b f(x) dx.

Worked examples

  1. ∫ x^2 dx = x^3/3 + C.
  2. Definite: ∫_0^1 x dx = 1/2.
  3. ∫ 3x^2 dx = x^3 + C (constant multiple).
  4. Compute ∫_0^2 x^3 dx = [x^4/4]_0^2 = 16/4 = 4.
