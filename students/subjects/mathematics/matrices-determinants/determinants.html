Determinants

Determinant of 2×2 and 3×3 matrices, properties of determinants and using determinants to check invertibility.

Worked example

det([[a,b],[c,d]]) = ad - bc. For [[1,2],[3,4]] = 1·4 - 2·3 = -2.

Practice

  1. Find determinant of [[2,1],[1,2]].
  2. Is [[1,2],[3,4]] invertible? (brief check).

Brief solutions

  1. det = 4 - 1 = 3.
  2. det = -2 ≠ 0 so invertible.
