Matrices

Matrix notation, addition, scalar multiplication, matrix multiplication (conformability) and the identity matrix.

Worked example

Add: [[1,2],[3,4]] + [[0,1],[1,0]] = [[1,3],[4,4]].

Practice

  1. Compute product: [[1,0],[0,1]] × [[2,3],[4,5]].
  2. Find scalar multiple: 3 × [[1,2],[3,4]].

Brief solutions

  1. Product = [[2,3],[4,5]] (identity property).
  2. [[3,6],[9,12]].
