Ratios, Proportions & Rates

Understanding proportional relationships, unit rates and scaling problems.

Worked example

If 5 apples cost $3, 8 apples cost? → unit price = 3/5 = 0.6 → 8 × 0.6 = $4.8.

Practice

  1. Solve for x: 4/5 = x/15.
  2. If car travels 150 km on 10 L, how far on 7 L?

Brief solutions

  1. x = 15 × 4/5 = 12.
  2. Unit rate = 15 km/L → 7 × 15 = 105 km.
