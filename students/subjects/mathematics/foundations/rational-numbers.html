Rational Numbers

Fractions that can be expressed as a ratio of integers. Convert between fractions and decimals, simplify, and compare.

Worked example

Convert 3/8 to decimal: 3 ÷ 8 = 0.375.

Practice

  1. Simplify 45/60 and write in lowest terms.
  2. Express 0.2 as a fraction.

Brief solutions

  1. 45/60 = 3/4.
  2. 0.2 = 1/5.
