Variations

Direct and inverse variation, proportionality constants and modelling simple relationships.

Worked example

If y varies directly as x and y = 12 when x = 3, find y when x = 5. k = y/x = 4 → y = 4×5 = 20.

Practice

  1. y varies inversely as x; y = 6 when x = 2. Find y when x = 3.
  2. Identify whether the following is direct or inverse: speed and time for fixed distance.

Brief solutions

  1. k = yx = 12 → when x=3, y = 12/3 = 4.
  2. Speed and time for fixed distance are inversely proportional.
