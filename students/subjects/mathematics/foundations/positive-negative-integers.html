Positive & Negative Integers

Master the fundamental operations with positive and negative integers - a crucial foundation for algebra and advanced mathematics.

Learning Objectives

  • • Understand and represent integers on a number line
  • • Compare and order integers (including absolute value)
  • • Perform addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division with integers
  • • Apply integer operations to solve real-world problems

1 What are Integers?

Integers are whole numbers that can be positive, negative, or zero. They do not include fractions or decimals.

Integers = {..., -3, -2, -1, 0, 1, 2, 3, ...}

Negative Integers Zero Positive Integers

The Number Line

Numbers increase as you move right, decrease as you move left

Key Concept

Numbers further to the RIGHT on the number line are GREATER. Numbers further to the LEFT are SMALLER.

Example: -2 is greater than -5 (because -2 is to the right of -5 on the number line)

2 Absolute Value

The absolute value of a number is its distance from zero on the number line, regardless of direction. It is always positive or zero.

|a| = the absolute value of a

|-5| = 5

Distance from 0 is 5 units

|7| = 7

Distance from 0 is 7 units

3 Addition and Subtraction Rules

Addition Rules

  • Same signs: Add the absolute values, keep the sign
  • (-3) + (-5) = -8
  • Different signs: Subtract the smaller absolute value from the larger, keep the sign of the larger
  • (-7) + 4 = -3

Subtraction Rule

Key: Subtracting is the same as adding the opposite!

  • 8 - (-3) = 8 + 3 = 11
  • -5 - 7 = -5 + (-7) = -12
  • -2 - (-6) = -2 + 6 = 4

Problem: Calculate -15 + 8 - (-12) + (-5)

Step 1: Convert all subtractions to additions

-15 + 8 + 12 + (-5)

Step 2: Group positive and negative numbers

Positive: 8 + 12 = 20
Negative: -15 + (-5) = -20

Step 3: Add the results

20 + (-20) = 0

Answer: 0

4 Multiplication and Division Rules

Sign Rules for Multiplication and Division

(+)(+) = +

Pos × Pos = Pos

(-)(-) = +

Neg × Neg = Pos

(+)(-) = -

Pos × Neg = Neg

(-)(+) = -

Neg × Pos = Neg

Memory Tip

"Same signs = Positive, Different signs = Negative"

Like signs attract (become positive), unlike signs repel (become negative)!

Problem: Calculate -3 × (4 - 7) + 5

Step 1: Solve inside the brackets first (BODMAS)

(4 - 7) = -3

Step 2: Perform the multiplication

-3 × (-3) = 9

Step 3: Complete the addition

9 + 5 = 14

Answer: 14

Problem: Calculate (-48) ÷ (-6)

Apply the sign rule: Negative ÷ Negative = Positive

(-48) ÷ (-6) = 48 ÷ 6 = 8

Answer: 8

Real-World Applications

Temperature

Temperatures can be positive (above freezing) or negative (below freezing).

If it's -5°C and rises by 8°C: -5 + 8 = 3°C

Banking

Positive = deposits/credits, Negative = withdrawals/debts

Account: -₦500 + ₦1000 = +₦500

Elevation

Above sea level = positive, Below sea level = negative

Dead Sea: -430m, Mt. Everest: +8,849m

Sports

Goal difference: Goals scored (positive) vs. conceded (negative)

+15 - 8 = +7 goal difference
