Mathematics

Structured learning order — start with Foundations of Numbers & Operations.

1. Foundations of Numbers & Operations

Positive & negative integers, fractions, ratios and basic financial arithmetic.

2. Number Systems & Logic

Bases, indices, logarithms, surds and basic logic.

3. Algebraic Foundations

Expressions, equations, functions and polynomials.

4. Geometry & Trigonometry

Shapes, mensuration, coordinate geometry and trigonometry.

5. Matrices & Determinants

Matrices, determinants and applications to linear systems.

6. Calculus

Differentiation and integration with applications.

7. Statistics & Probability

Data representation, measures, permutations and probability.
