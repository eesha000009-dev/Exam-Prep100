Regulation & Coordination

Why Study This?

Explains how organisms sense and respond to internal/external change — essential for behaviour and physiology topics later.

Topics

  • Regulation of the internal environment (homeostasis basics: temperature, pH, water)
  • Nervous coordination (neuron structure, synapse, reflex arcs)
  • Sensory receptors and organs (eyes, ears, taste, touch basics)
  • Hormonal (endocrine) coordination integrated with nervous system

Key Practicals

  • Reflex/response time experiments
  • Model nerve impulse demonstrations
  • Simple sensory tests (visual acuity, response to stimuli)
