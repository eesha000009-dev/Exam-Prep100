Micro-organisms & Health

Exploring the world of microorganisms and their impact on human health

Division Overview

This division explores the fascinating world of microorganisms and their relationship with human health. You'll learn about different types of microorganisms, their roles in disease and health, and their practical applications in biotechnology.

Micro-organisms and Health

Study disease transmission, immunity, vaccines, and antibiotics

Applications

Learn about fermentation and basic biotechnology applications

Learning Path

1. Foundation

Begin with understanding different types of microorganisms and their basic characteristics

2. Health Impact

Learn how microorganisms affect human health and our defense mechanisms

3. Practical Applications

Explore how we use microorganisms in beneficial ways through biotechnology
