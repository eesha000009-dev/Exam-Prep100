Key Concepts

1. Homeostasis Principles

  • • Control systems:
    • - Receptors (detecting change)
    • - Control center (processing)
    • - Effectors (responding)
  • • Feedback mechanisms:
    • - Negative feedback
    • - Positive feedback
    • - Set points and ranges

2. Temperature Regulation

  • • Heat balance:
    • - Heat production
    • - Heat loss mechanisms
    • - Behavioral responses
  • • Control mechanisms:
    • - Vasodilation/vasoconstriction
    • - Sweating
    • - Shivering
  • • Adaptations in different organisms

3. pH Balance

  • • Buffer systems:
    • - Carbonic acid-bicarbonate
    • - Phosphate buffers
    • - Protein buffers
  • • Acid-base balance:
    • - Respiratory control
    • - Renal control
  • • pH disturbances and compensation

4. Water Regulation

  • • Osmoregulation:
    • - Water potential
    • - Osmotic balance
  • • Control mechanisms:
    • - ADH regulation
    • - Kidney function
    • - Thirst mechanism
  • • Adaptations in different environments
