Regulation & Coordination

Understanding how organisms maintain internal balance and respond to their environment

Division Overview

This division explores how living organisms maintain their internal environment and coordinate responses to external changes. From basic homeostatic mechanisms to complex nervous and hormonal systems, you'll learn about the intricate control systems that keep organisms functioning optimally.

Learning Path

  1. 1.

  2. 2.

    Study Nervous Coordination

    Learn about the fast-acting nervous system and its components.

  3. 3.

    Explore Sensory Systems

    Examine how organisms detect and respond to environmental stimuli.

  4. 4.

    Complete with Hormonal Coordination

    Understand the endocrine system and its integration with nervous control.
