Key Concepts

1. Gas Exchange Principles

  • • Requirements for efficient exchange:
    • - Large surface area
    • - Thin exchange surface
    • - Good blood supply
    • - Concentration gradient
  • • Diffusion of gases
  • • Ventilation mechanisms

2. Human Respiratory System

  • • Structure:
    • - Airways (trachea, bronchi)
    • - Lungs and alveoli
    • - Chest cavity
  • • Breathing mechanism:
    • - Inspiration
    • - Expiration
    • - Muscle involvement

3. Gas Transport

  • • Oxygen transport:
    • - Hemoglobin
    • - Oxygen dissociation
  • • Carbon dioxide transport:
    • - Dissolved CO₂
    • - Bicarbonate
    • - Carbaminohemoglobin

4. Comparative Gas Exchange

  • • Fish gills
  • • Insect tracheae
  • • Plant stomata
  • • Adaptations to different environments
