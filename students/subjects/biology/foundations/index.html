Foundations of Biology

Classification & Cell Biology: The fundamental building blocks of life

Why Study This First?

Cells and classification are the language and units of biology. Without a firm cell-level understanding, physiology and ecology won't make sense.

Prerequisites:

Basic chemistry (water, macromolecules) — if absent, we'll provide a short recap.
