Key Concepts

1. Levels of Organization

  • • Cells → Tissues → Organs → Organ Systems → Organism
  • • Integration of systems
  • • Specialization at each level

2. Types of Tissues

  • • Epithelial tissue:
    • - Protection and secretion
    • - Types and locations
  • • Connective tissue:
    • - Support and connection
    • - Blood, bone, cartilage
  • • Muscle tissue:
    • - Movement and contraction
    • - Types of muscle
  • • Nervous tissue:
    • - Signal transmission
    • - Neural networks
