Comparative & Applied Biology

Exploring biological diversity and its practical applications

Division Overview

This division integrates various aspects of biology, comparing different organisms' systems and exploring how biological knowledge is applied in real-world situations. From comparative anatomy to practical applications in agriculture and conservation, you'll discover how biological principles work across different scales and contexts.

Topics

Supporting Systems and Comparative Anatomy

Compare anatomical structures and support systems across different organisms

Adaptations

Study behavioral, structural, and physiological adaptations

Applied Biology

Explore applications in agriculture, health, conservation, and biotechnology

Discover how biological knowledge is applied in various fields
