Key Concepts

1. Skeletal Systems

  • • Vertebrate Skeletons:
    • - Bone structure
    • - Joint types
    • - Vertebral modifications
    • - Limb adaptations
  • • Invertebrate Support:
    • - Exoskeletons
    • - Hydrostatic skeletons
    • - Shell structures
  • • Plant Support:
    • - Cell walls
    • - Vascular tissues
    • - Mechanical tissues

2. Circulatory Systems

  • • Open Circulation:
    • - Hemolymph
    • - Sinuses
    • - Simple hearts
  • • Closed Circulation:
    • - Blood vessels
    • - Heart chambers
    • - Blood types
  • • Plant Vascular Systems:
    • - Xylem
    • - Phloem
    • - Transport mechanisms

3. Respiratory Systems

  • • Aquatic Respiration:
    • - Gills
    • - Countercurrent exchange
    • - Surface diffusion
  • • Terrestrial Respiration:
    • - Lungs
    • - Tracheal systems
    • - Book lungs
  • • Plant Gas Exchange:
    • - Stomata
    • - Lenticels
    • - Internal spaces

4. Nervous Systems

  • • Simple Networks:
    • - Nerve nets
    • - Basic responses
    • - Sensory structures
  • • Complex Systems:
    • - Central nervous system
    • - Peripheral nerves
    • - Specialized organs
  • • Plant Responses:
    • - Hormonal control
    • - Tropisms
    • - Signal transmission
