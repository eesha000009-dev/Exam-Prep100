Genetics & Evolution

Understanding inheritance, variation, and evolutionary change

Division Overview

This division explores how characteristics are passed from one generation to the next and how species change over time. From Mendel's fundamental principles to the basics of DNA and natural selection, you'll learn about the mechanisms that drive biological inheritance and evolution.
