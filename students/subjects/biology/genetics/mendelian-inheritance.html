Key Concepts

1. Mendel's Laws

  • • Law of Segregation:
    • - Allele pairs separate
    • - Random distribution to gametes
    • - Pure breeding examples
  • • Law of Independent Assortment:
    • - Different traits inherit separately
    • - Random combinations
  • • Experimental evidence:
    • - Pea plant characteristics
    • - Breeding patterns

2. Chromosomes and Inheritance

  • • Chromosome structure:
    • - DNA packaging
    • - Gene locations
  • • Meiosis connection:
    • - Chromosome separation
    • - Gamete formation
  • • Chromosomal basis:
    • - Gene pairs
    • - Allele segregation

3. Dominance Patterns

  • • Complete dominance:
    • - Dominant alleles
    • - Recessive alleles
    • - Phenotype expression
  • • Genotype vs Phenotype:
    • - Homozygous
    • - Heterozygous
  • • Common examples:
    • - Human traits
    • - Plant characteristics

4. Genetic Crosses

  • • Punnett squares:
    • - Setting up crosses
    • - Predicting outcomes
  • • Types of crosses:
    • - Monohybrid cross
    • - Dihybrid cross
    • - Test cross
  • • Probability rules:
    • - Product rule
    • - Sum rule
