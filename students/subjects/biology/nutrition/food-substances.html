Key Concepts

1. Carbohydrates

  • • Types:
    • - Monosaccharides
    • - Disaccharides
    • - Polysaccharides
  • • Functions: Energy source, storage, structure
  • • Testing: Benedict's and iodine tests

2. Proteins

  • • Structure:
    • - Amino acids
    • - Peptide bonds
    • - Protein structure levels
  • • Functions: Growth, repair, enzymes
  • • Testing: Biuret test

3. Lipids

  • • Types:
    • - Fats and oils
    • - Phospholipids
  • • Functions: Energy storage, insulation
  • • Testing: Emulsion test

4. Vitamins & Minerals

  • • Fat-soluble vitamins (A, D, E, K)
  • • Water-soluble vitamins (B, C)
  • • Essential minerals
  • • Deficiency diseases
