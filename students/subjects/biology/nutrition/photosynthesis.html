Key Concepts

1. Overview & Requirements

  • • Basic equation: 6CO₂ + 6H₂O → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂
  • • Requirements:
    • - Light energy
    • - Carbon dioxide
    • - Water
    • - Chlorophyll

2. Light Reactions

  • • Light absorption by chlorophyll
  • • Photolysis of water
  • • ATP production
  • • NADPH formation

3. Dark Reactions (Calvin Cycle)

  • • Carbon fixation
  • • Reduction
  • • Regeneration
  • • Glucose synthesis

4. Limiting Factors

  • • Light intensity
  • • Carbon dioxide concentration
  • • Temperature
  • • Water availability
  • • Chlorophyll concentration
