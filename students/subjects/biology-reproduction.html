Reproduction, Development & Growth

Why Study This?

Links cell/organ systems with life cycle continuation and population dynamics.

Topics

  • Sexual reproduction: systems & behaviours (human reproductive anatomy, gametogenesis)
  • Development of new organisms / Fruits (fertilization, seed/fruit formation)
  • Growth stages, metamorphosis where relevant

Key Practicals

  • Dissection or models of reproductive organs
  • Observation of germination and seedling growth
  • Mapping life cycles
