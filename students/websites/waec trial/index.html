About WAEC Practicals

This simulator covers topics from the WAEC syllabus and WAEC practical examinations for physics, Biology and Chemistry from 1988 to 2025, providing students with hands-on experience in scientific experimentation. Each simulation is designed to replicate real laboratory conditions and procedures used in actual WAEC examinations.
