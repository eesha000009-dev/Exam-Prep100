## Academic Programs
The school offers comprehensive education across multiple levels:
- **Creche**: Ages 3 months - 2 years
- **Nursery**: Ages 2 - 5 years (Nursery 1, 2, 3)
- **Primary School**: Ages 5 - 11 years (Primary 1-6)
- **Secondary School**: Ages 11 - 17 years (JSS 1-3, SS 1-3)
