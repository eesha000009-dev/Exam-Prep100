Welcome to Girau International School

Where Learning Meets Excellence

10+ Years of Excellence

Taking Learners to Higher Ground

At Girau International School, we are committed to providing world-class education that nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and character development.

Located in the heart of Millennium City, Kaduna, our school offers a unique blend of Nigerian and British curriculum, preparing students for both local and international opportunities. Our state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated educators create an environment where every child can thrive.

Learning Through Play

We believe in holistic education that develops the whole child - academically, socially, and physically.

Our students engage in a variety of activities including sports like ping-pong and soccer, cultural celebrations, and academic competitions. This balanced approach ensures they develop into well-rounded individuals ready to face the challenges of the future.

With our school bus services and safe learning environment, parents can rest assured their children are in good hands from the moment they leave home until they return.

Cultural Day

Celebrating Nigeria's Rich Heritage

Embracing Our Cultural Heritage

Our annual Cultural Day celebration showcases the beautiful diversity of Nigerian culture.

Students proudly display traditional attire from various ethnic groups including Igbo, Yoruba, Nupe, Hausa, Igala, and Kanuri cultures. This vibrant celebration fosters unity while celebrating diversity.

Through music, dance, traditional attire, and cultural displays, our students learn to appreciate and respect the rich cultural tapestry that makes Nigeria unique.
