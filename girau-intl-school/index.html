why choose us

Why Choose Blue Horizon Schools

Well-rounded Education

A well-rounded education empowers a child with the essential skills to navigate complex situations—sharpening intellect, boosting cognitive abilities, enhancing social interactions, and mastering presentation skills, just to name a few.

Secured Environment

With Partnership with local security agencies, we provide 24/7 surveillance with CCTV, three layers of perimeter fencing, strategic security posts, and over 20 operatives, ensuring top-level security at all times.

We Prepare for the Future

This machine age of today is driven by advancements in cyber space, Artificial Intelligence (AI), robotics and digital platforms. That is why BHS offers an enriching programme in coding, robotics, drone technology for children ages 8 – 16, to prepare our children for this future.

Established in 2024, the BHS is duly registered under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) 1990 , with the Corporate Affairs Commission. Its location is unique, because of the serenity of the environment, the ease with which it can be accessed, and the spacious and secure facilities in the fastest-growing urban parts of Kaduna Metropolis.
