Who We Are

The Ultimate Learning Journey

Girau International School (GIS) is a premier educational institution in Kaduna, Nigeria, designed according to the best standards of excellence for teaching, learning, and character development. Our curriculum, facilities, and personnel meet the ideal standards for education and human capital development.

Located in the heart of Millennium City, we offer a unique blend of Nigerian and British curriculum, preparing students for both local and international opportunities. Our state-of-the-art facilities and dedicated educators create an environment where every child can thrive.
