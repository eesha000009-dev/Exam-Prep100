How It Works

Seamless Student-Teacher Workflow

Our platform streamlines the entire learning process from assignment creation to grade monitoring.

Step 1

Assign Work

Teachers create and assign tasks, quizzes, and projects to students with due dates and instructions.

Step 2

Submit Assignments

Students complete and submit their work online with file attachments and written responses.

Step 3

Grade & Feedback

Teachers review submissions, provide grades, and give detailed feedback for improvement.

Step 4

Track Progress

Both students and teachers monitor academic progress with detailed analytics and reports.

Powerful Features

Everything you need for effective teaching and learning in one integrated platform.

Assignment Management

Create, distribute, and collect assignments effortlessly. Support for multiple file formats and automatic deadline tracking.

Grading System

Efficient grading tools with rubrics, inline feedback, and automatic grade calculations. Export grades to reports.

Progress Monitoring

Real-time dashboards showing student performance, completion rates, and areas needing improvement.

Communication Hub

Built-in messaging system for teacher-student communication. Announcements, discussions, and Q&A forums.

Resource Library

Access textbooks, video lessons, past questions, and study materials organized by subject and topic.

Smart Notifications

Stay updated with assignment deadlines, new grades, announcements, and upcoming events via notifications.
