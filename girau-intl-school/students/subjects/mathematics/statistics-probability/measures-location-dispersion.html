Measures of Location & Dispersion

What to measure

Mean (average), median (middle), mode (most frequent) describe location. Range, IQR, variance and standard deviation describe spread.

Boxplot (visual)

Boxplots show median, quartiles, and outliers. Example below for dataset [1,2,3,4,10].

Worked Examples

  2. Dataset [5,7,9,2]: range = 9 - 2 = 7.
