What You Need

Admission Requirements

Required documents for enrollment

General Requirements

  • Completed admission form
  • 2 recent passport photographs
  • Birth certificate (original and photocopy)
  • Previous school report/transcript
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
  • Immunization record

Age Requirements

  • Creche: 3 months - 2 years
  • Nursery: 2 - 5 years
  • Primary 1: 5 - 6 years
  • Primary 2-6: Appropriate age progression
  • JSS 1: 10 - 12 years
  • SSS 1: 13 - 15 years

Frequently Asked Questions

The admission process involves obtaining and completing the admission form, submitting required documents, taking an entrance examination, and upon successful completion, receiving an admission letter. The entire process is designed to be straightforward and transparent.

Girau International School follows a blended Nigerian and British curriculum. This unique approach ensures students are well-prepared for both local examinations (WAEC, NECO) and international opportunities.

School hours are from 7:30 AM to 4:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Students are expected to arrive by 7:30 AM for morning assembly. After-school activities and extra lessons may extend to 5:00 PM.

Yes, the school provides safe and reliable transportation services covering various routes within Kaduna. Our buses are well-maintained and driven by experienced, trained drivers.

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports (football, basketball, athletics), clubs (debate, drama, music, art), and special programs like public speaking, coding, and leadership development.
