WAEC Physics Syllabus

Complete guide to practical physics experiments and requirements

Required Practical Experiments

Mechanics

  • Simple Pendulum - Determination of acceleration due to gravity
  • Hooke's Law - Investigation of the extension-load relationship
  • Principle of Moments - Verification using meter rule
  • Equilibrium of Forces - Using force board

Optics

  • Refraction through glass prism
  • Focal length determination of converging lens
  • Reflection and refraction of light

Electricity

  • Ohm's Law verification
  • Resistors in series and parallel
  • Use of potentiometer

Required Equipment

Mechanics Equipment

  • Meter rules
  • Stopwatches
  • Spring balances
  • Masses and weight hangers

Optics Equipment

  • Glass blocks
  • Optical pins
  • Protractors
  • Ray boxes
