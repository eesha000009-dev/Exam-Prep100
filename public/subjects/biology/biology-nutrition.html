Key Concepts in Nutrition

Autotrophic Nutrition:

Organisms that make their own food (plants, algae, some bacteria)

Heterotrophic Nutrition:

Organisms that obtain food from other organisms (animals, fungi)

Photosynthesis Equation:

6CO₂ + 6H₂O + light → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂

Balanced Diet:

Contains all nutrients in correct proportions for good health
