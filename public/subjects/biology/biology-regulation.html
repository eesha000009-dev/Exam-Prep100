Introduction to Regulation

Regulation involves the coordination and control of body functions through nervous and hormonal systems. It maintains homeostasis and allows organisms to respond to their environment.

Key Concepts in Regulation

Negative Feedback:

A mechanism that reverses changes to maintain homeostasis

Nervous vs Hormonal:

Fast, short-term (nerves) vs Slow, long-term (hormones)

Insulin & Glucagon:

Regulate blood glucose - insulin lowers, glucagon raises

ADH:

Anti-diuretic hormone controls water reabsorption in kidneys
