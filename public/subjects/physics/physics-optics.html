Key Concepts in Optics

Mirror/Lens Formula:

1/f = 1/u + 1/v

Snell's Law:

n₁ sin θ₁ = n₂ sin θ₂

Power of Lens:

P = 1/f (in diopters when f in meters)

Critical Angle:

sin c = n₂/n₁ (for TIR)
