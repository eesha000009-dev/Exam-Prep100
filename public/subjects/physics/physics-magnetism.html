Key Concepts in Magnetism

Magnetic Poles:

North and South - like poles repel, unlike poles attract

Right-Hand Grip Rule:

Determines direction of magnetic field around current-carrying wire

Faraday's Law:

Induced EMF is proportional to rate of change of magnetic flux

Motor Effect:

Force on current-carrying conductor in magnetic field: F = BIL
