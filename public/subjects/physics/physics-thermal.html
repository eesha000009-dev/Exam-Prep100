Key Concepts in Thermal Physics

Ideal Gas Equation:

PV = nRT = NkT

Specific Heat Capacity:

Q = mcΔT

Latent Heat:

Q = mL (fusion or vaporization)

First Law of Thermodynamics:

ΔU = Q - W
